We’re thrilled to offer an exciting programme of events across the UK and beyond in the coming months – including our flagship event to celebrate our 30th year: Tough Mudder at Belvoir Castle.

## What is Tough Mudder?

Taking place on Saturday 4 and Sunday 5 July 2026 this unforgettable challenge features over 12 obstacles across either a 5K or 15K course. It’s not about competition or timing – it’s about teamwork, determination and plenty of mud. You’ll tackle the course alongside friends, teammates and fellow participants, all working together to earn the iconic orange headband.

## Support Dove Cottage’s 30th Anniversary

To mark this special milestone, Dove Cottage Day Hospice is offering a limited number of charity places for just £30 per person – a saving of over £75 compared to registering directly with Tough Mudder. It’s our way of celebrating 30 years of providing compassionate palliative care to local families living with life-limiting conditions.

In return, we ask each participant to commit to raising a minimum of £250 in sponsorship. Charity places come at a cost to us, and your fundraising ensures we can cover that cost while generating vital income to support our services. Every pound you raise will help us continue to care for those who need us most.

## Secure your place today

Sign up today, take on the challenge, and make a real difference. Your support will help Dove Cottage Day Hospice continue to provide comfort, dignity and expert care to people and families across our community.
