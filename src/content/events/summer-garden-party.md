Our Summer Garden Party brings supporters, families, and local businesses together for a relaxed afternoon in aid of Dove Cottage.

## What to expect

- Live acoustic music
- Tea, cakes, and refreshments
- Raffle and small fundraising games
- Information about volunteering and local support services

If you would like to attend or help on the day, please use our contact form and our team will be in touch.
